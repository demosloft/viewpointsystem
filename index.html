Frequently Asked Questions

Who will I be talking to?

You'll generally be talking with someone with a very technical background because we're a technically-focused company (even our marketing guy has a masters in electrical engineering). You might talk to our main sales guy, who has a PhD in electrical engineering, or directly with one of our engineers.

What will we talk about?

The main focus will be on working toward solving your problem technically. First, you'll answer questions that help us understand your requirements, then we'll briefly touch on the business side (budget, timing, fit).

When will I hear from you?

You'll definitely hear from us within 1 business day. But more often than not, you'll actually get contacted within an hour or two to schedule a time to chat. You'll answer a couple of additional questions at that time, to offer up some context.

Likely Topics for Discussion

  • Test type: Is this for production test or product validation?
  • Application: What is the use case?
  • I/O: How many inputs and outputs? Rates? Voltage levels? Resolution? Interfacing to what type of sensor?
  • Functions: What functionality/processing algorithms are needed?
  • Data Storage: HOw much data needs to be logged? How does it need to be stored?
  • GUI: What are your user interface needs? How will you interact with it?
  • Budget: Do you have the budgete for everyting you want, or do you prefer a phased approach?
  • Timeline: When do you want to start? When do you need to be done?
